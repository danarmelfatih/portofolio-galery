ABOUT ME

"The Story Behind the Lens"

"Hello, I'm Danar Melfatih Kustantoro. Photography for me isn't just a hobby, but a way of telling stories. Every photo I take is a piece of time that I want to preserve and share."

For me, one picture can say more than a thousand words. Through this gallery, I want to invite you to see the world from my perspective—seeing the simple beauty, the small details, and the moments that are often overlooked.

This gallery contains journeys, memories, and stories that I capture through light. I hope each photo here can inspire you, inspire you, or even remind you of a precious moment in your life.
